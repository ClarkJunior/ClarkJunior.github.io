League Of Legends

Today we are going to talk about the game League of Legends. First off its a MOBA game which also stands for Multiplayer Online Battle Arena. League of Legends is a very competitive and fast-paced game. It has a blend of RPG and RTS elements. Two teams with unique champions battle it out in the Field Of Justice in different maps and gamemodes. The creaters constantly expand the game with new, constant updates and more feautures. There are also tournaments between different teams all across the world which are held every year. With this said League Of Legends offers endless gameplay for players of every skill level.
